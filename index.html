EXPLORE    M O N S T E R   M A N I A



In   M O N S T E R   M A N I A, you gotta prove you're the best monster out there, even if you're just a slime! Destroy the goblins before they destroy you, survive the various waves os enemies and become the last monster standing or else the fight never ends!


You can access the game here: https://github.com/jessicasouza-dev/Game


You can watch the game here: https://youtu.be/cbO-5CIZ30A



DESTROY THE GOBLINS

Kill every goblin that are attacks you! Beware, their heavy machinery gets heavier every wave!


The basic goblins can you hurt with touch! The shooter goblins will shoot at you every time you are in their view! And the sniper goblins, they will shoot at you through the walls!



GET POWER-UPS

Increase your powers after every wave and guarantee your continuous survival! Or else, you'll be stuck in an endless fight forever!


You can increase your speed, recover your health, multiply your shots and rise your damage! You just gotta choose wisely!
